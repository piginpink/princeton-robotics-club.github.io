JOIN THE CLUB!

You can build a robot. It’s not as hard as you might think, and it’s especially easy and fun when you’re a part of a team.


Join

Joining is as simple as letting us know that you’re interested. Princeton University undergraduate students from all majors and years can join. There are no pre-reqs or tests to get into the club. In fact, most of our officers had never built a robot before being in the club. So just send us an email using the link below saying you’re interested in joining. We’ll help you find your place in the club.
